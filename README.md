### 🎓 CampusBot – Rule-Based Student Assistant Chatbot  
**Live:** https://campus-buddybot.vercel.app  
**GitHub:** https://github.com/AkashB29/campusbot  

CampusBot is a **rule-based chatbot** designed for college students. It requires **no AI model, no API keys, and no internet**—just predefined smart keyword matching and structured responses.  

**What It Covers:**  
- **Academics:** Essay writing, research tips, exam prep, citations, focus & motivation  
- **Campus Life:** Making friends, budgeting, dorm life, time management, mental health  
- **Bureaucracy:** Enrollment, student IDs, transcripts, scholarships, visas  
- **Entertainment:** 37+ PG jokes including dad jokes, riddles & one-liners  

**Tech Stack:** Next.js
